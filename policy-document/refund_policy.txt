REFUND AND CANCELLATION POLICY
TransitFlow Transit Authority

1. NATIONAL RAIL — REFUND POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1.1 Standard Refund Window
National Rail tickets may be refunded up to 24 hours before the scheduled
departure time. A processing fee of 10% of the original ticket price will
be deducted from all approved refunds. The remaining amount will be
returned to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

1.2 Same-Day Cancellations
Tickets cancelled within 24 hours of the scheduled departure time are
non-refundable under standard conditions. Exceptions are granted only in
the following circumstances:
  - Documented medical emergency affecting the ticketed passenger
  - Proven service cancellation or significant delay (over 60 minutes)
    caused by TransitFlow operations
  - Bereavement of an immediate family member, with supporting documentation

1.3 First Class Tickets
First class ticket refunds follow the same 24-hour window rule. However,
the processing fee for first class refunds is waived if the cancellation
is made more than 72 hours before departure. First class tickets cancelled
between 24 and 72 hours before departure incur the standard 10% fee.

1.4 Service Disruption Refunds
If a National Rail service is cancelled or delayed by more than 60 minutes
due to operational issues, passengers are entitled to a full refund with
no processing fee. Passengers may submit a disruption refund claim through
the TransitFlow app or at any staffed station, including Central Station
(NR01), Parklands (NR02), and Riverside Park (NR03), within 14 days of
the affected travel date.

1.5 Partial Journey Refunds
If a passenger is unable to complete their journey due to a service
disruption, a partial refund will be calculated based on the number of
stops not travelled. The refund amount equals the per-stop rate multiplied
by the number of remaining stops from the point of disruption to the
original destination.

2. METRO — REFUND POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 Single Tickets
Metro single tickets are non-refundable once purchased. This applies to
all metro lines including M1, M2, M3, and M4. Passengers are advised to
confirm their journey details before completing a purchase.

2.2 Day Passes
Metro day passes may be refunded if the request is made within 2 hours
of purchase and the pass has not been used for any journey. Once a day
pass has been activated (i.e., used for at least one trip), it becomes
non-refundable regardless of remaining trips on the same day.

2.3 Service Disruption Refunds
If the metro network experiences a full service suspension lasting more
than 30 minutes, passengers who have purchased a single ticket or day
pass during the affected period are entitled to a full refund. Claims
must be submitted within 7 days of the disruption through the TransitFlow
app or at staffed interchange stations such as Central Square (MS01).

3. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 Via the TransitFlow App
Log in to your account, navigate to "My Bookings," select the relevant
booking, and tap "Request Refund." You will receive a confirmation email
within 24 hours. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days.

3.2 At a Staffed Station
Refund requests can be submitted in person at any staffed National Rail
station. Passengers must present their booking ID and a valid photo ID.
Staffed stations include Central Station (NR01), Greenfield Junction (NR04),
and North Terminal (NR05).

3.3 Required Information
All refund requests must include:
  - Booking ID (format: BK-XXXXXX for National Rail)
  - Registered email address
  - Reason for cancellation
  - Supporting documentation (if applicable for exceptions)

4. SPECIAL PASSENGER CATEGORIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 Children (Under 5)
Tickets purchased for children under 5 years of age are fully refundable
at any time before the travel date, with no processing fee.

4.2 Senior Citizens (65 and over) and Students
Discounted tickets purchased by senior citizens or students are subject
to the same refund policy as standard tickets. The 10% processing fee
applies. Proof of eligibility (senior card or valid student ID) must have
been presented at the time of purchase for the discounted fare to be valid.
